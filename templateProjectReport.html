5. Limitations & Future Improvements

5.1 Limitations

Current Constraints:
  • Battery Life: The device currently lasts only 6 hours on a 2000mAh battery.
  • Connectivity: The system fails if Wi-Fi signal drops below -80dBm.
  • Enclosure: The 3D printed case is not waterproof.

5.2 Future Improvements

To make this product market-ready, we propose the following upgrades:

  1. Deep Sleep Mode: Implementing ESP32 Deep Sleep could extend battery life to 3 months.
  2. PCB Design: Replacing the breadboard with a custom PCB to reduce size and loose wires.
  3. LoRa Integration: Using LoRa instead of Wi-Fi for long-range agricultural use.
